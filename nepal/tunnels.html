Churia Tunnel

1. Nagdhunga Tunnel Location: Connects Kathmandu Valley to the western Terai region. Length: Approximately 2.68 km . Status: Under construction (as of 2024). Purpose: Eases traffic congestion at Nagdhunga Pass; shortens travel time and improves safety. Funding: Supported by the Government of Japan (JICA). 2. Siddhababa Tunnel Location: On the Butwal–Palpa road section (Siddhartha Highway). Length: Approx. 1.2 km (planned). Status: Under planning/construction. Purpose: Avoids landslide-prone areas and ensures road connectivity year-round.
