Deposit, Types of Deposits & Deposit Mobilization

What is Deposit?

A deposit refers to the amount collected by bank and financial institutions from its customer with or without paying interest, with the condition of refund after specific time period or upon the request order from the customers. It is one of the primary sources of funds for banks and plays a crucial role in the financial system. Deposits may be fixed, saving, current, etc.

According to BAFIA 2073, "Deposits means amounts deposited in current, saving or fixed accounts of a bank or financial institution accepts through such various financial instruments as prescribed by the Nepal Rastra Bank."

Deposits are liabilities for banks because they must return the money to customers when demanded.

Types of Deposits

Deposits can be categorized into two following category.
A. Interest Bearing Deposit
B. Non-interest Bearing Deposit

A. Interest Bearing Deposit

a. Saving Deposits

The saving deposits that are made on saving accounts. The purpose of saving deposit is to encourage saving habit of commpon people. Saving deposits attract interest normally less than fixed deposit.

According to BAFIA-2073, the saving account means an account of amounts, deposited in a bank for saving purposes.
Saving accounts are generally opened for the saving of customers (individuals, nonprofit organization, charitable trusts, clubs, associations and cooperative) who want to save for meeting future needs and wants.

Demand deposits can be withdrawn at any time without prior notice. These are highly liquid and commonly used for daily transactions.

  1. Interest Bearing
  2. Flexible Interest Rate
  3. Restriction or limitation in deposit and withdrawal

b. Fixed/Term Deposit

Money is deposited for a fixed term with higher interest rates for a fixed period of time is Fixed or term deposit. The rate of interest on this account is higher than other accounts. The fixed term may be for three to five years generally depends upon the bank and financial institutions or Nepal Rastra Bank policy.

According to BAFIA 2073, fixed account means the account of amount deposit in a bank for a certain period of time.
