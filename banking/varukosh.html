Varu (IC) Kosh

Varu Kosh is like note kosh. Varu kosh is for Indian Currency and note kosh is for Nepalese Currency. Just like note kosh central bank maintain varu kosh in government commercial bank i.e. Rastriya Banijya Bank and Nepal Bank Limited of that area where there is lack of its own branch for the purpose of helping bank and financial institutions of that area for easy IC fund management or liquidity management.

As there is open border system between Nepal and India, lots of Nepalese people and businessman visit India for business transactions, daily retail shopping as well as treatment and entertainment purpose. Nepal Rastra Bank is helping general public to business man by maintaining varu kosh and make it easier for Indian Currency Exchange.
