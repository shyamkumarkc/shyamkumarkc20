Retail Banking

Retail banking is doing banking business with individual customers, in a broader sense, it refers to dealing of commercial banks with individual customers, both on the assets (personal loans, housing, auto loans and educational loan) and liabilities (Fixed, Current, Saving account) sides of the balance sheet. Banks to diversify their portfolio and to diversify their portfolio risk. 

Retail Products:

A. Retail Deposit Products:

  • Saving Account
  • Call Account
  • Current Account
  • Term Deposit Account
  • Recurring Deposit Account

B. Retail Loan Products:

  • Home Loan
  • Auto Loan
  • Consumer Loan
  • Personal Loan
  • Educational Loan
  • Credit Card

C. Retail Services:

  • Safe Deposit Locker
  • Depository Services
