CG Pay Wallet

CG Pay is a provider of services, payment gateways and mobile wallets in Nepal. Launched in January 2020, CG Pay is an emerging online payment solution in Nepal. It allows users to pay for a wide range of services such as basic utility payments, hotel bookings, movies and domestic airfares, events and more. It is operated and owned by Nepal Payment Solution Pvt. Ltd.

CG Pay provides payment solutions to hundreds of sellers across Nepal and provides seamless payments using e-banking, smart banking, cards and wallets. Not only limited to mobile app users, but CG Pay also has more than 8000 POS and Agent Network offers CG Pay service to end-users.
